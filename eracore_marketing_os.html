One Asana project per channel (e.g. Kasun's video board, Javeria's SEO board). The OS is the source of truth for stage — pushing a task moves its card to the matching column. Asana never moves a card back. New tasks created directly in Asana still get pulled in. Create a token at app.asana.com/0/my-apps. Stored locally only.

Map each team member to their Asana user GID so tasks are assigned automatically when pushed. Get GIDs from your Asana workspace URL when viewing a member's profile, or via the Asana API.
